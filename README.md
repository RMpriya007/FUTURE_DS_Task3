About This Project

This project analyzes real world marketing funnel data to understand
how potential customers move through different stages from initial
contact to final conversion. The analysis was completed as part of
the Future Interns Data Science and Analytics Internship 2026.

The goal of this project is to help a business identify where
customers are dropping off in the funnel, which channels bring
the best quality leads, and what actions can be taken to improve
the overall lead to customer conversion rate.



Business Problem

A bank conducted multiple marketing campaigns to convince customers
to subscribe to term deposit products. Despite running large scale
campaigns with thousands of contacts, the overall conversion rate
remained very low. The business needed a clear data driven analysis
to understand which segments convert best and where the funnel
needs improvement to maximize return on marketing investment.



Key KPI Results

Total Leads              : 45,211
Converted Customers      : 5,289
Overall Conversion Rate  : 11.70 percent
Average Campaign Calls   : 2.76 calls per customer

These numbers show that 88.30 percent of leads are currently
dropping off without converting which represents a major
opportunity for improvement in the marketing funnel.



Dashboard Overview

The Power BI dashboard contains 5 visual components built
with a professional purple theme for maximum visual impact.

Chart 1 - Conversion Overview
A bar chart comparing total leads versus converted customers
showing the clear conversion gap in the marketing funnel.

Chart 2 - Conversion by Age Group
A chart showing which age groups have the most leads and
which age segments are most likely to convert successfully.

Chart 3 - Conversion by Job Type
A horizontal bar chart showing blue collar and management
job types have the highest number of leads in the funnel.

Chart 4 - Conversion by Contact Method
A pie chart showing cellular contact method dominates at
64.77 percent making it the most effective outreach channel.

Chart 5 - Conversion Trend by Month
A line chart showing May had the highest campaign activity
and how conversion volumes changed across all 12 months.



Key Insights

Insight 1 - Low Conversion Rate
Only 11.70 percent of total leads are converting which means
88.30 percent of marketing spend is not generating returns.
This needs immediate attention and funnel optimization.

Insight 2 - Cellular is the Best Channel
Cellular contact method reaches 64.77 percent of all contacts
making it the most effective and preferred outreach channel.
Telephone and unknown methods have significantly lower reach.

Insight 3 - Seasonal Campaign Patterns
May month recorded the highest campaign activity and leads.
There is a clear seasonal pattern in marketing performance
that the business should leverage for future campaigns.

Insight 4 - Job Segment Distribution
Blue collar and management job types represent the largest
lead segments showing these are the primary target audiences
for the bank marketing campaigns.

Insight 5 - Campaign Call Fatigue
The average of 2.76 calls per customer suggests that multiple
follow up calls are needed to convert a lead which may also
be causing customer fatigue and increasing drop off rates.



Recommendations

Recommendation 1
Focus the majority of marketing budget on cellular contact
method since it has the highest reach at 64.77 percent and
likely the best conversion potential among all channels.

Recommendation 2
Plan and intensify marketing campaigns around May month when
customer engagement is historically at its peak to maximize
lead generation and conversion during the best period.

Recommendation 3
Create highly targeted campaigns specifically for blue collar
and management job segments since they represent the largest
portion of the lead pool in the marketing funnel.

Recommendation 4
Reduce the number of follow up calls per customer and instead
focus on improving the quality of each interaction to reduce
customer fatigue and improve the overall conversion rate.

Recommendation 5
Build a mid funnel nurturing strategy to convert more of the
interested leads into paying customers and push the overall
conversion rate well above the current 11.70 percent baseline.
